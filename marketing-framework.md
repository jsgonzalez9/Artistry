## 7. RED FLAGS & STOP CONDITIONS

### Pause & Assess Immediately If:
- CAC exceeds product price (spending more than earning)
- 30+ days with zero sales after $10k+ spend
- Landing pages below 2% conversion for 2+ weeks
- Email open rates below 15% consistently
- Cost per click exceeds $6 for 5+ days

### Optimize If:
- CAC between $2,000-3,000 (marginal but fixable)
- Close rate below 2% (sales process issue)
- Lead quality declining (audience mismatch)

### Scale If:
- CAC below $2,000 on consistent sales
- ROAS above 2:1
- Organic traffic growing 20%+ MoM
